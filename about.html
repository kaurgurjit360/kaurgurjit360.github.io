About Me

Hey there. My name is Gurjit Kaur. Obviously. I was born on September 6, 1998 in a little room in India. So technically, you could say i’m “homemade”... It’s okay though, I look it as just one of the things that make me unique and special. After having enough pollution to last us a lifetime, my family thought it would be best if we moved to the land of opportunities also known as America. At the age of 10, I had traveled across the world ready to face the world. Of Course that attitude changed after first 5 minutes of middle school. There were so many obstacles to overcome. But, I am not one to back away from challenges. I began working even harder. At school and at home.I progressed each year, from 620 to 715 to 810. By the end of middle school, I had become the top student in my class, won a science fair and began helping other struggling students who were native to this country. My philosophy is that when faced by an obstacle, one should not spend their time thinking about the cause of their problem but instead think about what they can do from that point on. One should lay out their options and decide from there. My practical and realistic viewes continously help me overcome challanges.
